Game Rules

Know the rules. Live longer.

ğŸ‘¥ Basic Mechanics

  • All players start as humans in a protected zone
  • A small number of players are randomly selected as original zombies
  • Humans are "tagged" by zombies and become zombies
  • Zombies can be "stunned" by humans with a Nerf blaster or sock
  • The game continues until humans are overwhelmed or time runs out

ğŸ”« Human Rules

  • Humans must have a blaster or sock at all times outside safe zones
  • Blasters must be Nerf brand or equivalent foam dart guns
  • Ammunition must be foam darts - no bullets allowed
  • Humans must wear a white headband to identify themselves
  • Tagging a zombie requires hitting them with a dart from 20+ feet away
  • Stunning a zombie with a sock requires touching them while holding the sock
  • You cannot tag a zombie from indoors or in safe zones
  • Teachers, staff, and classroom spaces are off-limits for zombie tags

ğŸ§Ÿ Zombie Rules

  • Zombies must wear a red headband to identify themselves
  • To tag a human, a zombie must physically touch them
  • Tagged humans become zombies within the hour
  • Stunned zombies must sit out for 15 minutes before resuming play
  • Zombies cannot enter safe zones (specific buildings and times)
  • Zombies cannot tag humans during lunch hours (specific times)
  • Zombies must maintain a human-like appearance and cannot use masks

  • Zombies cannot enter safe zones under any circumstances
  • Zone status may change during the game
