Survival Guide

Tips, tricks, and strategies to dominate the game

üéØ Human Survival Strategies

Early Game

  • Form alliances with other humans immediately
  • Identify safe zones and use them strategically
  • Scout the campus to find escape routes
  • Stock up on ammunition at the start
  • Don't venture out alone - stay with groups

Mid Game

  • Establish a "home base" in a safe zone near resources
  • Set up watch rotations to monitor zombie movements
  • Create distraction tactics to draw zombies away from objectives
  • Maintain constant communication with your team
  • Don't trust new players - they could be turned zombies
  • Ration your ammunition - don't waste shots

Late Game

  • Defend key strategic locations
  • Keep only your most trusted team members close
  • Make a final stand if necessary - go out fighting!
  • Track zombie locations and plan accordingly

üßü Zombie Dominance Guide

Early Game (Original Zombie Phase)

  • Coordinate with other original zombies
  • Target isolated humans away from safe zones
  • Build momentum early by making converts
  • Don't give your position away immediately
  • Use the element of surprise to your advantage

Mid Game (Growing Horde)

  • Delegate leadership among veteran zombies
  • Organize coordinated rushes on human strongholds
  • Use new zombies as disposable scouts
  • Learn to anticipate human defense patterns
  • Force humans out of safe zones through attrition
  • Create fake retreats to lure humans into ambushes

Late Game (Overwhelming Force)

  • Coordinate the final siege on remaining human positions
  • Don't let humans regroup - maintain pressure
  • Eliminate the last pockets of resistance
  • Celebrate your undead victory!

‚ö° Pro Tips

For Everyone

  • Know the Campus: Memorize key locations, shortcuts, and safe zones
  • Gear Matters: Invest in quality equipment - it makes a difference
  • Communication: Use Discord, text signals, or agreed-upon meeting spots
  • Physical Fitness: Cardio is essential - you'll be running a lot
  • Weather Prep: Check forecasts and prepare accordingly
  • Dress Appropriately: Wear comfortable clothes you don't mind getting messy
  • Hydration: Bring water bottles - stay hydrated during play
  • Respect Boundaries: Know which areas are off-limits

Human-Specific Tips

  • Practice your aim - accuracy matters when zombies charge
  • Keep a low profile when moving between safe zones
  • Use decoys to test zombie patrol patterns
  • Form specialized teams: scouts, guards, ammo-runners
  • Keep detailed notes on zombie sightings

Zombie-Specific Tips

  • Move quietly - humans can hear you coming
  • Use your numbers to isolate targets
  • Learn to take a hit and keep going
  • Don't waste energy chasing armed humans - wait for better opportunities
  • Support your zombie brethren - tag more humans

üèÖ Winning Tips

  • Never Give Up: Against the odds? That's when legends are made
  • Adapt Quickly: The game is constantly changing - stay flexible
  • Know Your Role: Play to your strengths, not everyone can be a frontline warrior
  • Intelligence: Gather intel on enemy positions and movements
  • Morale: Keep your team's spirits up - psychology is half the battle
  • Patience: Sometimes waiting is better than acting
  • Teamwork: No individual can win - you need your squad
  • Have Fun: Remember, it's a game. Enjoy the experience!

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Playing alone - safety in numbers!
  • Wasting ammunition on zombies you can't hit
  • Ignoring safe zone schedules (for humans)
  • Overestimating your abilities - zombies are fast
  • Spreading yourself too thin across campus
  • Trusting new allies too quickly

  • Getting caught outside during zombie rushes
  • Not staying hydrated and fed
  • Giving up mentally before the game ends
